Ingredients

  • 5+ Thai chilies (adjust to preferred spice level)
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 small onion diced
  • 2 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 Tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 Tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 Tbsp water
  • 1.5 tsp sugar
  • 300g ground beef, pork, or chicken
  • 1.5 cup fresh Thai basil leaves
  • Cooking oil as needed
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cups of cooked jasmine rice

Steps

  1. Mince Thai chilies, garlic and onion.
  2. Combine fish sauce, oyster sauce, soy sauce, water and sugar.
  3. In a wok or saute pan, add cooking oil and saute the chilies and garlic over medium high heat.
  4. Add ground meat and toss to break it up into smaller pieces.
  5. Add sauce continue tossing until the meat nearly done.
  6. Add onions and toss until the meat is cooked.
  7. Turn off the heat and stir in the Thai basil.
  8. In a wok or saute pan, heat up some cooking oil over medium high heat.
  9. Crack an egg into the hot oil and fry until edges are browned.
  10. Serve over rice and top it with the fried egg.
