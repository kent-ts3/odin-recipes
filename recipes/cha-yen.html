Ingredients

  • 3 Tbsp Thai tea
  • 1 cup boiled water
  • 1.5 Tbsp sweetened condensed milk
  • 2-3 tsp sugar
  • pinch of salt
  • 1-2 Tbsp evaporated milk
  • ice

Steps

  1. Steep Thai tea in hot water for 3-5 minutes.
  2. Combine condensed milk, sugar, and salt in a mixing glass.
  3. Once tea is ready, strain and pour the brewed tea into the glass and stir to dissolve the milk and sugar.
  4. Pour mixture in a glass filled with ice and drizzle the top with evaporated milk.
